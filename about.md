## About This Repository

This repository contains comprehensive study materials for the **CFA Level II** exam, organized by topic with detailed analysis and explanations.

### Purpose

The goal is to provide a well-organized, accessible reference for CFA Level II candidates studying:
- Corporate Issuers and Corporate Finance
- Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) considerations in investment analysis

### Structure

Materials are organized hierarchically:
- **Level II** - The exam level
- **Topics** - Major subject areas
- **Markdown Files** - Individual study documents

### Continuous Updates

This repository is regularly updated with new insights, clarifications, and additional materials to support CFA Level II preparation.

### Contributing

Feel free to use these materials for your CFA exam preparation!
