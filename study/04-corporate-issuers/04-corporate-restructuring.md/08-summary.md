A short closing summary. Here's the key points consolidated for exam use.

---

### Three Categories of Restructuring

| Category | Effect | Specific Types |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Investment** | ↑ Size/scope | Equity investments, joint ventures, acquisitions |
| **Divestment** | ↓ Size/scope | Sales, spin-offs |
| **Restructuring** | Same size, ↑ performance | Cost restructuring, balance sheet restructuring, reorganization |

---

### The Four-Phase Evaluation Process

| Phase | Key Output |
|-------|-----------|
| **1. Initial evaluation** | What/when/material/why — apply professional skepticism |
| **2. Preliminary valuation** | Comparable company, comparable transaction, premium paid analysis |
| **3. Modeling** | Pro forma financial statements |
| **4. Update investment thesis** | Revised valuation and recommendation |

> Only conduct the **full evaluation** for **material** restructurings.

---

### Materiality — Two Dimensions

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Size** | >10% of acquirer's pre-announcement EV = "large" |
| **Fit** | Alignment with analyst's expectations for the issuer's strategy |

---

### Valuation Methods Summary

| Method | Includes Control Premium? | Best For |
|--------|--------------------------|---------|
| Comparable company | ❌ No | Spin-offs |
| Comparable transaction | ✅ Yes (embedded) | Acquisitions/sales |
| Premium paid | ✅ Yes (explicit) | All transactions |

---

### WACC in Restructurings

$$WACC = w_d r_d(1-t) + w_p r_p + w_e r_e$$

Restructurings affect WACC through **both weights and costs:**

| Driver Type | Key Factors |
|------------|------------|
| **Bottom-up** | Stability, profitability, leverage, asset specificity |
| **Top-down** | Interest rates, credit spreads, ERP, regulatory environment |

---

### One-Page Mental Model for the Exam

```
Investment Actions → ↑ size
  Equity investment → minority stake, equity method
  Joint venture → shared control, equity method
  Acquisition → full control, consolidation
        ↓
Divestment Actions → ↓ size
  Sale → cash in, clean exit
  Spin-off → no cash, higher potential value
        ↓
Restructuring Actions → same size, ↑ performance
  Cost restructuring → ↑ margins (Year 1 often dilutive)
  Sale-leaseback → ↑ liquidity, ↓ debt, potential rating upgrade
  Reorganization → court-supervised debt relief

Evaluation process:
  Initial → material? (>10% EV + strategic fit)
  Preliminary → comps, transactions, premium paid
  Modeling → pro forma EPS, debt/EBITDA, WACC
  Update thesis → buy/sell/hold revised
```
