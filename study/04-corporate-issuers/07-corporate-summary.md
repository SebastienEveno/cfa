## Summary: Module 1 Wrap-Up — Analysis of Dividends & Share Repurchases (CFA Level II)

---

### Core Takeaways

**1. Dividend Safety Metrics**
- **Earnings coverage**: Sustainable if > 1.5x for most mature companies
- **FCFE coverage**: The superior metric; considers actual cash available
- **Red flags**: Declining coverage trends, high leverage, cutting capex to fund dividends

**2. Payout Policies**
- **Stable dividend policy**: Most common; based on sustainable long-term earnings
- **Lintner model**: Captures real-world practice of gradual dividend adjustment
- **Global trend**: Fewer payers but higher aggregate payouts; repurchases rising

**3. Share Repurchases**
- **Methods**: Open market most common; Dutch auction and fixed tender for speed
- **EPS impact**: Accretive only if shares bought below intrinsic value
- **Flexibility advantage**: Can be suspended; don't create commitment like dividends

**4. Valuation Equivalence**
- **Under equal conditions**: Dividends and repurchases are equivalent
- **In practice**: Tax differences and signaling create preference for one or the other
- **Wealth transfer risk**: Direct negotiation repurchases at premium harm other shareholders

**5. Strategic Choice**
- **Tax environment**: Influence payout method choice significantly
- **Investor base**: Institutional vs. individual investors have different preferences
- **Capital structure**: Target leverage should inform payout vs. debt reduction trade-off
- **Business cycle**: Uncertain times favor flexible repurchases over committed dividends

---

### Exam Focus Areas

✓ Calculate dividend coverage ratios and interpret sustainability
✓ Apply Lintner model to forecast dividend changes
✓ Understand repurchase methods and their implications
✓ Recognize valuation equivalence assumptions and when they break
✓ Defend payout policy choices based on company circumstances

### Key Formulas to Memorize

$$\text{Earnings coverage} = \frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Dividends}}$$

$$\text{FCFE} = \text{CFO} - \text{CapEx} + \text{Net borrowing}$$

$$\Delta \text{DPS} = (\text{Expected EPS} \times \text{Target payout ratio} - \text{Previous DPS}) \times \text{Adjustment factor}$$

$$\text{FCFE coverage} = \frac{\text{FCFE}}{\text{Dividends + Repurchases}}$$
